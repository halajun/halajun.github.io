# Short Bio

* I am a senior research staff at [IVC](https://www.tugraz.at/institute/icg/home), TU Graz, working in Prof. [Friedrich Fraundorfer](https://www.tugraz.at/institute/icg/research/team-fraundorfer/people/friedrich-fraundorfer/)'s group. Prior to this, I was a post-doc researcher hosted by [RPL](https://www.kth.se/is/rpl), KTH, Sweden. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Robotics and Vision, from the Australian National University (ANU). My research interests includes robot perception, visual SLAM and 3D vision, using standard/event cameras for UAVs/UGVs, multi-beam/side-scan sonars for AUVs, etc.
